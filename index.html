Sacred Crystals & Their Energies

Discover the transformative power of these sacred stones, each aligned with one of the five Chinese elements to bring balance and harmony to your life.

Amethyst

Water

The amethyst is known for its spiritual protection and purification properties. It enhances intuition and promotes emotional balance, making it ideal for those seeking to connect with their higher selves.

Rose Quartz

Earth

The stone of unconditional love, rose quartz opens the heart chakra to all forms of love. It's particularly beneficial for healing emotional wounds and attracting loving relationships.

Citrine

Fire

Known as the merchant's stone, citrine attracts wealth, prosperity, and success. It carries the power of the sun, energizing every level of life and enhancing creativity.

Black Tourmaline

Earth

A powerful protective stone that repels and blocks negative energies. It's excellent for psychic protection and grounding, creating a shield around the aura.

Clear Quartz

Metal

The master healer, clear quartz amplifies energy and thought. It enhances spiritual growth, clarity of mind, and can be programmed for any intention.

Lapis Lazuli

Water

The stone of wisdom and truth, lapis lazuli stimulates enlightenment and enhances intellectual ability. It's excellent for activating the third eye.

Carnelian

Fire

A stone of motivation and endurance, carnelian boosts vitality and stimulates creativity. It's particularly beneficial for overcoming procrastination.

Jade

Wood

Known as the dream stone in Chinese culture, jade brings harmony and balance. It attracts good luck and friendship while stabilizing the personality.

Pyrite

Metal

Called fool's gold, pyrite is a stone of wealth and abundance. It enhances memory, stimulates intellect, and protects against negative energies.

Malachite

Wood

A stone of transformation, malachite absorbs negative energies and pollutants. It encourages risk-taking and change while protecting during travel.
